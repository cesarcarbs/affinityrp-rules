AffinityRP Rulebook

Community Values

AffinityRP aims to create a welcoming and story-driven roleplay environment. Players should prioritize creativity, respect, and immersive storytelling.

18+ Requirement

Players must be 18 years or older to play on the server. Exceptions may only be granted by management.

Microphone Requirement

All players must have a working, quality microphone.

English Requirement

Players must be able to speak and write English to participate.

Character Names

Character names must be realistic and sensible. You may not roleplay as a real-world celebrity or notable figure.

Toxicity

Toxic behavior on any platform related to the community (Discord, Twitch, Reddit, etc.) may result in removal from the server.

Stay In Character

Players must remain in character at all times while on the server.

RDM / VDM

  • Killing another player without engaged roleplay is prohibited.
  • Vehicles may not be used as weapons.

New Life Rule

When your character becomes unconscious you forget events leading up to that moment until you are revived.

Powergaming

Using game mechanics or external information to gain an unfair roleplay advantage is not allowed.

Metagaming

Using information obtained outside the game (streams, Discord calls, etc.) to influence roleplay is prohibited.

Robbery Rules

  • You cannot rob communication devices.
  • You cannot rob players who are AFK.
  • You cannot force ATM withdrawals.

FearRP

Players must value their character’s life and respond realistically to threats or dangerous situations.

Server Restart Rules

  • No crimes 30 minutes before restart.
  • Wait 30 minutes after restart before committing crimes.

Driving Rules

  • Vehicle stunts must fit the RP situation.
  • Excessive unrealistic driving is not allowed.
  • Major crashes must be roleplayed realistically.
