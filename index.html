AffinityRP Rulebook

Community Key Values

Too often, roleplay communities feel toxic or unwelcoming to newcomers. That is not who we are. We are building a place where characters grow, stories unfold, and players create moments worth remembering.

This community focuses on semi-serious, story-driven roleplay. Staff may assess RP quality through community complaints and determine punishments case-by-case.

  • Gunplay first roleplay is not allowed.
  • You must be 18 years or older to play (exceptions by management).
  • A working microphone is required.
  • You must speak and write English.
  • Character names must be realistic.
  • No default characters without RP Manager approval.
  • No toxicity anywhere (Discord, Twitch, Reddit, etc).
  • Remain in character at all times.
  • Do not radio while downed or cuffed.
  • Rule reports must include video evidence.
  • Hate speech, harassment, sexism, or discrimination is prohibited.

1.0 Communication & Conduct

1.0 Communication

This is an English-speaking server.

Voices cannot be used to identify someone wearing a face covering.

1.1 Respect The Community

Your actions should never remove enjoyment from another player. Sending angry OOC messages over in-game issues is not allowed. Handle issues through tickets.

1.2 Staff Rulings

All staff rulings are final. If you believe a ruling is incorrect, open a ticket with proof. Do not DM or tag staff members.

1.3 Lying to Staff

Lying to staff in tickets, chat, or any other communication will result in disciplinary action.

1.5 Exploits & Cheats

  • No cheats, scripts, or modifications that give an advantage.
  • Exploiting bugs for gain is prohibited.
  • Bugs must be reported when discovered.
  • AFK exploit farming will result in punishment.

1.7 Zero-Tolerance Policy

  • Rape or sexual assault RP is prohibited.
  • Suicide RP is not allowed.
  • Violations result in immediate bans.

1.8 Combat Rules

1.8 Deathmatching (RDM / VDM)

  • Killing without engaged roleplay is prohibited.
  • Starting fights without RP buildup is not allowed.
  • Vehicles cannot be used as weapons.
  • PIT maneuvers are allowed.
  • Accidents must be roleplayed with EMS or police.

1.9 Downed & New Life Rule

Phase 1 (Knocked Down)

You may still speak but must roleplay injury.

Phase 2 (Unconscious)

  • No speaking or radio use.
  • Only /me communication allowed.
  • You forget events leading to unconsciousness.
  • Memory loss may last up to 30 minutes.

2.0 Roleplay Rules

2.0 Powergaming & FailRP

  • Using mechanics to gain unfair advantage is prohibited.
  • Teleport markers may only be used if accessible to others.
  • Wait 10 minutes before storing a chase vehicle.
  • Wait 10 minutes before changing clothes after crimes.
  • You must provide federal ID when requested.

2.1 Metagaming

Using out-of-character information for roleplay advantage (stream sniping, Discord calls, etc.) is prohibited.

2.4 Robbing & Kidnapping

  • You cannot rob communication devices.
  • You cannot force ATM withdrawals.
  • You cannot rob AFK players.
  • You cannot rob players inside menus.

2.9 FearRP

  • You must value your character's life.
  • If outnumbered at gunpoint you must comply.
  • You may flee in a vehicle but risk being shot.

3.0 Server Policies

3.0 Perma-Death

Players cannot be forced into permanent death unless approved by gang rules or Department of Justice sentencing.

3.2 Server Restart Rules

  • No crimes within 30 minutes before restart.
  • Wait 30 minutes after restart before committing crimes.
  • Scenes must resume after unexpected restart.

3.7 Gangs & Organizations

Gangs must provide quality roleplay. Low-quality RP may result in gang strikes or disbandment. All gangs must submit applications.

4.0 Driving

Driving Rules

  • Vehicle stunts must fit the roleplay situation.
  • Excessive unrealistic driving is prohibited.
  • Major crashes must be roleplayed realistically.
